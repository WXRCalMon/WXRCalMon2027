# The 2027 Weather Radar and Calibration Workshop

### Scope
Exchange of experiences and future plans on methods and tools for (DualPol) Weather Radar—operation, monitoring, and calibration.

### Topics
Contributions welcome in:
* **Calibration**: Reflectivity and dual polarization approaches
* **Electro/Mechanical**: Pointing accuracy, transmitters, …
* **New Technology**: SSPA, remote calibration, signal sources, polarization bias control, AI
* **Network Threats**: Wind turbines, interference, and more
* **Monitoring & Processes**: Latest methodologies and tools
* **Ongoing Issues**: Challenges and solutions

### Practical information
* **Dates**: 13-15 October 2027
* **Location**: Royal Netherlands Meteorological Institute (KNMI), De Bilt, the Netherlands (hybrid format)

### Registration
* **Registration** opens in 2027 at WXRCalMon.github.io/WXRCalMon2027
* **Fee**: Free (Participants cover their own expenses)
* **On-site Capacity**: Limited to 60 participants

### Workshop Format
The workshop inccludes the following components:
* Oral presentations
* Poster sessions
* Discussions

The intended audience of the workshop consists of:
* National Meteorological Services (NMS)
* Radar manufacturers
* Researchers
